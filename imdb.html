imdb user profile redesign

A redesign mockup I made as a part of a design challenge where I was supposed to design one UI component per day for 100 days.

Filename: device.png

Filesize: 1.4 MB

Click on the image to see it in full size.

The design challenge was to design a user profile for any device and product/brand.

As I am an avid movie-fan and love to hang out on IMDb, I had a look at their profile pages. As I feel they look very outdated and uninspiring, I decided to see what I could do about that.

I wanted to use a large image and make the whole site feel a little more like a social network for movies rather than just a fancy border for your ratings list.

As it came out, I like how the key statistics really stand out and allows you to brag a little about how much of a movie geek you are.
