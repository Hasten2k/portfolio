big sur california

As a personal challenge, I set about redesigning bigsurcalifornia.org. A website owned by the Chamber of Commerce in Big Sur.

Filename: index.html

Filesize: 10 KB

Click on the image to see it in full size.

After spending two weeks in California in august 2015 and driving down Highway One, I really was stricken by how amazing an experience the drive really was. Because of that, I thought it would be fun to see if I could translate that feeling to the site.

You can see the result here and decide for yourself what you think. If I were to do anything differently, I would spend even more time looking for the perfect images and also spend some more time on interactivity and motion design.
