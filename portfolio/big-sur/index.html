Welcome to

Big Sur

This is the official, complete, and definitive travel guide to all lodging, camping, restaurants, gift shops & art galleries in Big Sur; The ultimate travel resource and guide to California's central coast and your complete Big Sur Visitors Bureau.

Wildflowers always seem to be blooming, California Condors are flying overhead throughout Big Sur. California Sea Otters are rafting in the kelp beds. These are just a few of the natural wonders that make Big Sur a great place to visit. Come on out and watch the natural magic unfold before your eyes, and create your own.

From Big Sur luxury hotels and spas to rustic creekside campgrounds we believe Big Sur offers The Best in a wide variety of possibilities and we think you'll agree. Join us for a romantic getaway and come share with us one of the most spectacular coastlines in the world.

Where to

Stay

Big Sur offers numerous opportunities for lodging. Everything from luxry hotels and spas to camp sites. All with unprecedented views of the majestic California landscape.

If you are lucky, there might just be an opportunity to purchase your own piece of real estate in this magical place.

What to

Do

Big Sur is arguably one of the most beautiful places on earth. It also features activities and eperiences for everyone. From relaxing spas and hikes in the serene forests, to whale watching, mountain biking and surfing.

Or why not host an event in one of the most spectacular settings available? Anything from weddings to conferences and meet-ups are possible.
